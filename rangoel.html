Fresh City Farms
S01E03 Ran Goel
View Transcript

Ran is the CEO & Founder of Fresh City, a Toronto urban farm and organic retailer. Some of his formative childhood experiences include his grandmother’s stories about growing up on a Kibbutz, feeling democracy awaken in his childhood home of South Africa and his mom’s stuffed peppers. Ran has spoken on food issues before hundreds of audiences. He proudly leads a team of over 250-staff as they collectively create a better life through food. Before founding Fresh City, Ran practiced investment law in New York City for several years.
