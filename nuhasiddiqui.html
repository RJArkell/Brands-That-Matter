Eco Packers
S01E05 Nuha Siddiqui
View Transcript

Nuha Siddiqui is the Co-Founder and CEO or Eco-packers, are a start up based in Toronto that is looking to revolutionize how we manufacture single use plastic in a 100% eco-friendly and non-toxic way. Starting off as just a university project, Nuha and her team have been able to make significant advances in the space and also secure funding to hopefully scale the company in a global way. Nuha is someone who doesn't see problems, she sees solutions. No matter how big the obstacle, she's always ready to tackle it with a sense urgency.

During the interview, Nuha shared a great perspective that she came to realize. Sustainability isn't about competition, it's about collaboration. Sometimes it requires doing business with non sustainable brands in order to maximize impact.
